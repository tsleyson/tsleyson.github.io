Manga-only Chapters

Only includes chapters which weren't adapted or which were cherry-picked for scenes. If the entire chapter was merged with another chapter to create an anime episode, those chapters do not appear here.

Aqua Navigation 01: The Water Planet Totally omitted from the anime, although we can assume the events happened offscreen, a year before the first episode of Aria: The Animation, when Akari arrived on Aqua and first met Alicia, President Aria, and the Mailman.
Aqua Navigation 02: The Guide on the Water Like Navigation 01, we can assume these events happened sometime before the start of the series, when Akari first met Aika.
Aqua Navigation 05: The Hill of Hope Akari refers to the events of this chapter in Aria the Natural Episode 16: Parting with That Gondola. She was promoted to Single in this chapter.
Aqua Navigation 10: Fireworks
Aria Navigation 01: Neo-Venezia
Aria Navigation 05: Vogare Longa
Aria Navigation 08: Voices of the Stars Partially adapted in Aria the Natural Episode 3: That Night of the Meteor Shower… In the manga, this chapter introduces Al. In the anime episode, the Undine girls already know Al, so we can assume something like this chapter happened offscreen in the anime.
Aria Navigation 11: The First Gale of Spring Introduces Alice. In the anime, Alice was introduced in Aria the Animation Episode 3: With That Transparent Young Girl…, adapts a few events from this chapter.
Aria Navigation 18: Flying Fish in the Sky Introduces Woody. Again, seems to have happened offscreen in the anime, since Akari already knows Woody when he appears in Aria the Animation Episode 4: That Undeliverable Letter…
Aria Navigation 29: Lost Child Introduces President Maa. The introduction of President Maa was added to Aria the Animation Episode 6: That Which You Want to Protect…, but most of the material from Navigation 29 was not included.
Aria Special Navigation (Vol. 8): A Girl's Heart As I remember it, Maa's gender was casually dropped in a conversation in the anime.

Anime-Only Episode

Many of the anime episodes added extra content. Many also differed from the manga chapter only by the presence of Ai. Those are not mentioned here; only episodes which substantially did not exist in the original manga are included in this table.

Aria the Animation Episode 1: That Wonderful Miracle… Introduces Ai. In the manga, Ai did not appear until the final chapter.
Aria the Animation Episode 3: With That Transparent Young Girl… Sort of a borderline case. A lot of the material was adapted from Aria Navigations 03 and 11, and some also comes from Aqua Navigations 06 and 10. The anime changed the scenario and added glue material.
Aria the Animation Episode 4: That Undeliverable Letter… Introduces Woody.
Aria the Animation Episode 12: That Soft Wish… Gives us a look into the history of Aqua that we mostly got through narration in the manga.
